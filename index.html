Jpanda y sus amigos

Hi! Welcome to our website!

This is a school project at The Churchill School we made to show a little more about who we are — Jpanda, Marc, Majo, and Emirace. On this site, you’ll find our biographies, pictures, hobbies, and some fun facts about each of us.

We thought it would be cool to create a place where you can learn more than just our names. You’ll find out what we like to do, what makes us happy, and even some silly or interesting things that make us unique.

Each of us has our own part of the site, so you can check out everyone one by one. We hope you have fun looking around and getting to know us better.

Thanks for visiting our page — enjoy!
