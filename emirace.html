Emirace

Who I am

Hi! I'm Emiliano Vera Chavero, and I live in Mexico City. I’ve lived here all my life with my parents, Carlos Vera and Laura Chavero. I go to The Churchill School and I’ve been there since I was a little kid. It’s the place where I’ve learned a lot and made great memories.

Hobbies

One thing you should know about me is that I really love sports especially basketball. I started playing when I was 12, and since then, I’ve fallen in love with the game. It’s not just a hobby anymore, it’s something I’m super passionate about. I practiced a lot, and thanks to that, I made it to the top 10 basketball players in Mexico City for my age group. That made me really proud!

Special moments

One of my favorite moments was when my team won the regional 3x3 FIBA OPEN Tournament. It was a big competition with really strong teams, so winning felt amazing. It helped me become a better player and a better teammate too.

Likes

I’ve also tried other sports, and they’ve helped me stay active and focused. But nothing beats basketball for me. I love how fast it is, how you have to think quickly, and how important teamwork is. And honestly, it’s just really fun to play!

When I’m not playing, I like watching games too. I’m a huge fan of Fc Barclona and the Golden State Warriors. I think their style and energy are awesome, and I look up to a lot of their players.

Basketball has taught me a lot — not just about playing, but about working hard, not giving up, and believing in myself. I think that’s the coolest part.
